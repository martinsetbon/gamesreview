Dungeon Fighter Online

Lots of fun


Dungeon Fighter Online is a 2D side scrolling action MMORPG that was originally created in 2005 by Neople, the game closed soon after and then the global version server re-open in 2015.
The distributor of the game for the global server is none other than the creators of the game, Neople.
The game graphics is "old school" style, but this is on purpose, Neople wants to use the player nostalgia of theyre favorite arcades games.
The combat is very "anime" style and fast pace,tons of skills and flashy moves and effects everywhere (althought you can set the opacity of the effects in-game for clearer view).
Leveling is very easy (up untill max lvl) and if you just follow the storyline, the difficulty is close to none, the real game really starts when you are at max level and you start gearing your caracter up.
The game has a stamina system that empty up pretty fast, on the real, its encouraging the players to create many caracters, as you get various benefits for doing so.

The managment of the game is honeslty high level, there is events every 2 weeks, rewarding players for sometimes just playing the game, updates every month or 2, constantly new contents, and no pay to win, you can play this games many years without spending a dollar.
This game is clearly for fun and fun only, if you don't mind the graphic style and side scrolling, it is a real pleasure !

Classes

The game currently has more than 60 classes that you can chose from !
From the Slayer with a badass sword or the Fighter coming straight out of a fighting game to the Priest fighting with a giant cross or even the Frost mage and the Vampire mage, this game has a class for everyone.
The classes are gender locked, but in a weird way - almost every classes has its counterpart from the other gender, but in the end its just another class,for exemple, you have the male slayer and the female slayer, but all of the subclass has different names and are very different in the end.
