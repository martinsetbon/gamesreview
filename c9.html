Continent of the Ninth Seal

Awesome gameplay


Continent of the Ninth Seal is an fast pace dungeon crawler action MMORPG, with more than 18 classes with a very different gameplay for each class.
The game is distributed by Webzen and was created in 2012 and as it looks a bit old in term of graphics, it was beautiful when it came out, now it is just average.
But the strenght of the game reside in its gameplay.
Each classes AND each subclasses are very differents from each others, there is tons of skills (shortcut and commands skills) and it may takes a bit of time to get use to your class and all of your skills because of the complexity (especially for people not used to this kindof keyboard playing type game) but once you do, it is a real pleasure !
The combats are really technicals and nervous, even tho the difficulty is very low untill you reach a certain level.

The game is a dungeon crawler so it is expected, even for leveling, to do the same dungeon, sometimes over and over, you can change difficulty for more challenge and less boredom.
The real problem of the game is the distributor, Webzen, even tho the game is really fun to play, Webzen make it really hard to stick to it.
Each update are very very very distants from each others, you can play 6 month without anything new, and some contents are made to be horribly long and boring to farm, and you can always make it better and faster by paying money.
The events are often money related, and even the graphic quality of the paying avatar is MUCH better than the gears that you get in-game, everything is made so player buy things out of frustration.

Classes

The game currently has 5 classes and about 4 subclass for each of them. The classic warrior, the archer-type caracters, the whichslayer, a female fighting with blades, the wizard, and the Mystic, a Loli type caracter fighting with a lance. The classes are gender locked and each of them have very different gameplay.

Caracter customization

The caracter customization is not very deep, you can only change a few things like the hair, the general expression, the color, and then the body shape, muscles and scale of your caracter.
